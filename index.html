Adobo Recipe
Ingredients:
  • 1 kg chicken or pork
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup water
Instructions:
  1. Marinate meat in soy sauce and garlic for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat a pan and brown the meat.
  3. Add vinegar, water, bay leaves, and pepper. Simmer until tender.
  4. Serve with rice and enjoy!
Tapsilog Recipe
Ingredients:
  • 500 g beef tapa
  • 2 cups garlic fried rice
  • 2 sunny-side-up eggs
Instructions:
  1. Marinate beef with soy sauce, calamansi, and garlic overnight.
  2. Fry beef until cooked.
  3. Serve with garlic rice and eggs.
Sisig Recipe
Ingredients:
  • 500 g pork belly
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 chilies, chopped
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
Instructions:
  1. Grill pork belly and chop into small pieces.
  2. Mix with onions, chilies, mayonnaise, and soy sauce.
  3. Serve on a sizzling plate.
